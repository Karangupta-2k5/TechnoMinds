About Us

Who We Are

Technominds is the premier IT club of the BCA Department, established to foster a culture of technological innovation and collaborative learning among students.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements by organizing workshops, hackathons, and tech talks that equip students with practical skills.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and collaborative projects.

Development

We focus on enhancing coding skills and software development practices.

Learning

We provide a platform for continuous learning and skill development.
